Cloth & Chalk — Fabric Yardage Calculator for Bespoke Menswear

Cloth & Chalk is a free fabric yardage calculator for bespoke tailors and their clients. Enter your body or finished measurements for a suit, sport coat, trousers, vest or shirt and get an accurate fabric estimate along with a to-scale cutting layout showing how the pattern pieces fit on the cloth.

Whether you are calculating yardage for a two-piece suit, a three-piece suit, a single sport coat, bespoke trousers or slacks, a waistcoat, or a dress shirt, Cloth & Chalk gives you the correct amount of fabric to order — no more, no less. Supports 54-inch and 60-inch fabric widths.
